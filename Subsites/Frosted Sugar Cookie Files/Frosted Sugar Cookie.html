How to Make Frosted Sugar Cookies

The Frosted Sugar Cookie, a sweet and yummy cookie that is my personal favorite. Today i'll teach you how to make one in under 2 hours:>.

Before starting, you will need these items :

Equipment :

      Whisk(or an electric mixer)
      Measuring cups
      A rubber spatula

Ingredients

      Flour
      White sugar
      Eggs
      Butter
      Vanilla
      Baking powder
      Salt
      Milk

Guide

  1. Prehead your over to 350°F
  2. Mix eggs, 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract, white sugar, and 1/4 cup milk in a bowl(frosting). Whisk flour, salt, and baking soda in a seperate bowl(dough).
  3. Roll dough out on a floured surface, cut it into shapes, and arrange cookies on baking sheets.
  4. Bake the dough in the oven for 10 minutes and let it cool.
  5. Mix butter, white sugar, 1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract, and 2 tablespoons of milk in a bowl until frosting is spreadable and fluffy.
  6. Apply frosting onto the cookie.

Congratulations, you have made some frosted sugar cookies. Enjoy!
