How To Make Chocolate Chip Cookies

The Chocolate Chip Cookie is one of the most well known cookies in the world. Today i'll be teaching you how to make one in under 30 minutes:>.

Before starting, you will need these items :

Equipment :

      A Medium Sized Mixing Bowl
      Stand Mixer or an Electic Hand Mixer
      A Rubber Spatula
      Parchment Paper
      Measuring Cups
      A Cooling Rack

Ingredients :

      All-purpose flour
      Butter
      Eggs
      Brown or granulated sugar
      Baking soda
      Chocolate chips (Any type of Chocolate will do:>)
      Vanilla extract
      Baking soda
      Salt

Guide
  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F.
  2. In the bowl, mix butter, granulated and brown sugar until it has a fluffy texture
  3. Scrape the sides of the bowl with a rubber spatula, and add in an egg and some drops of vanilla extract. Mix for atleast 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add in your favorite chocolate chips into the mix:>.
  5. Scoop the dough out into 1 1/2 tbsp portions and apply it on parchment paper.
  6. Bake @ 350°F for 8-10 minutes (or more) until the cookies begin to turn golden brown on the edges.
  7. After baking, allow for cookies to cool on the pan for atleast 5 minutes before transferring to a wire cooling rack.

Congratulations, you have made yourself some chocolate chip cookies. Enjoy!
